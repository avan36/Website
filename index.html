middle place · iPhone · iPad · Mac

A private place to process things.

middle place is a personal support app for the space between conversations — one continuous, private thread that remembers where you left off, so you never start the story over. Everything stays on your device and in your own iCloud. No accounts, no tracking, nothing sold.

Privacy research · privacy-tech-lab

Holding the web accountable.
